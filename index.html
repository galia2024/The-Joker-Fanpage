Name The Joker
Description Arch-nemesis of Batman, characterized by his clown-like appearance and sadistic sense of humor
Notable Abilities Criminal mastermind, expert chemist, uses weaponized props and toxins
First Appearance Batman #1 (April 25, 1940)
Creators Bill Finger, Bob Kane, Jerry Robinson
Occupation Supervillain, Criminal mastermind

What's Most Important to The Joker?

  • Batman: His arch-nemesis and the central figure in his life.
  • Harley Quinn: Initially his psychiatrist, she becomes his close accomplice and romantic interest.
  • The Red Hood: An alias used by the Joker before his transformation into the villain we know.
  • Chemicals: His transformation into the Joker was caused by falling into a vat of chemicals.
  • Playing Cards and Jokes: Signature elements of his character, symbolizing his love for chaos and unpredictability.
  • Gotham City: The primary setting for his criminal activities and battles with Batman.
